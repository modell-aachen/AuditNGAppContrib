---+ AuditNGAppContrib
---++ Installation Instructions

* Install AuditNGAppContrib and its dependencies
* The installation path would be _apps/Audit, so you have to do some customisations
* * Move web to desired level
* * Change path settings in preferences to:
* * * Preferences in AuditTemplate/WebHome.txt
* * * DeviationTemplate.txt
* * * MeasuresTemplate.txt
* * * AuditQuestionForm.txt
* * * QuestionTemplate.txt
* * * WebPreferences.txt
